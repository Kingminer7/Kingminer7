what is it?

five nights at spigg's is a fnaf fan game based around AstralSpiff (spigg) and his streams. download (not finished yet as of 3/21/23)

characters and mechanics

spigg

spigg takes on the role of chica/bonnie in fnaf 1. he starts and parts and service streaming, and once he leaves, he never returns.
to stop him, shut the door he is at. he is only visible on cams.

loaf

loaf takes on a new role. she crawls through the vents to your office. when your camera is up and she is at the vent, she turns off your power.
to turn it back on, you have to press the button on the computer. she can be stopped by the vent door. she is only visible on the vent cams.

clown

clown takes on the foxy role. you must play clown often, or clown will jumpscare you.
i even found a tts that is similar to clown for a death sound.

mapbot

mapbot appears in your office sometimes, and you have to take a map. it takes 2 seconds to take it. if you do not take it, you die after some time.

giga monty

giga monty roams the building. if he's at your doors, close it FAST. if he sees you, permanent aggro. he'll be at the door the rest of the night.
